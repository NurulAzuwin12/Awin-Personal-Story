So, who am I?

My name is Nurul Azuwin binti Mohd Zawawi, but most people call me Awin. I was born on 12 December 2005 and was raised in Ipoh, Perak. A little about my lovely state in Peninsular Malaysia, Perak, is a true hidden gem, celebrated for its breathtaking natural beauty and captivating cultural heritage. Present-day Perak is a delightful blend of tradition and modernity, boasting a vibrant atmosphere fueled by its friendly and welcoming people. I love growing and staying in Perak because it has many "healing" places and is peaceful.

I'm a person who appreciates life's simple moments. I'm also a person who loves to laugh at even small things, and I'm a happy-go-lucky person. I find joy in reading novels, but sometimes a reading slump appears for a very long time. the most favourite genre of novels I like is romance, like from hate to love. hehe. I also love photography, especially capturing sweet snaps with my family, cousins, cats, and friends.
